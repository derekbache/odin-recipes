Breakfast Sandwich

Description

Bacon Egg and Cheese deliciousness

Ingredients

  • English Muffins
  • Canadian Bacon
  • American Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Heat skillet
  2. Fry Canadian Bacon, put aside
  3. Toast english muffins
  4. Fry Egg
  5. Assemble sandwich, muffin/egg/bacon/cheese/muffin.
